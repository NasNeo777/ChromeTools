概述

DeepSeek 工具箱（下称「本扩展」）是一款帮助用户对 YouTube、哔哩哔哩视频进行 AI 总结、翻译与批判性分析的浏览器扩展。我们高度重视您的隐私。开发者本人不会收集、存储或上传您的任何个人数据。本扩展不含任何分析、追踪或广告代码。

我们处理哪些数据

  1. 您的 DeepSeek API Key
    由您自行填写，仅保存在您浏览器本地（chrome.storage.sync）。它只用于由您的浏览器直接向 DeepSeek 接口发起请求，不会被发送给本扩展的开发者或任何其他第三方。若您登录了 Chrome 同步账号，该设置会由 Chrome 在您自己的设备之间同步（这一同步由 Google 提供，开发者无法访问）。
  2. 视频字幕与评论内容
    仅当您在视频页面主动点击本扩展的功能按钮时，本扩展才会读取当前视频的字幕文本及热门评论，并将其发送至 DeepSeek 接口以生成总结 / 翻译 / 分析结果。这些内容不会被本扩展存储，也不会发送给开发者，仅在生成结果的过程中即时传输。
  3. 您的偏好设置
    例如总结风格、输出语言、所选模型，仅保存在您浏览器本地，用于记住您的使用偏好。

我们不收集以下任何信息：姓名、邮箱、账号密码、地理位置、浏览历史、设备标识符，或任何用于识别您个人身份的数据。

第三方服务

本扩展的核心 AI 功能依赖 DeepSeek（深度求索）提供的 API。当您主动触发功能时，视频字幕 / 评论会由您的浏览器直接发送给 DeepSeek 进行处理。该数据的处理受 DeepSeek 自身隐私政策约束，请参阅：https://www.deepseek.com/。除 DeepSeek 外，本扩展不与任何其他第三方共享数据。

数据存储与保留

本扩展处理的所有本地数据（API Key 与偏好设置）均存储在您的浏览器中。卸载本扩展即会一并删除这些数据。字幕 / 评论内容不被本扩展持久化存储。

数据安全

所有网络请求均通过 HTTPS 加密传输。

我们不会做的事

  • 不出售、出租或交易您的任何数据；
  • 不将数据用于广告、画像或与功能无关的用途；
  • 不在后台收集数据，所有数据处理均由您主动操作触发。

儿童隐私

本扩展并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

Overview

DeepSeek Toolbox ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users summarize, translate, and critically analyze YouTube and Bilibili videos using AI. We take your privacy seriously. The developer does not collect, store, or upload any of your personal data. The Extension contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising code.

What Data We Handle

  1. Your DeepSeek API Key — entered by you and stored only locally in your browser (chrome.storage.sync). It is used solely for your browser to call the DeepSeek API directly and is never sent to the developer or any other third party. If you are signed into Chrome Sync, this setting is synced by Chrome across your own devices (handled by Google; the developer has no access).
  2. Video subtitles and comments — only when you actively click a feature button on a video page does the Extension read the current video's subtitle text and top comments and send them to the DeepSeek API to generate a summary / translation / analysis. This content is not stored by the Extension and is never sent to the developer; it is transmitted only transiently to produce the result.
  3. Your preferences — such as summary style, output language, and selected model, stored only locally to remember your settings.

We do not collect your name, email, credentials, location, browsing history, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The Extension's core AI functionality relies on the DeepSeek API. When you trigger a feature, video subtitles/comments are sent directly from your browser to DeepSeek for processing, subject to DeepSeek's own privacy policy (https://www.deepseek.com/). The Extension shares data with no other third parties.

Data Storage & Retention

All local data (API Key and preferences) is stored in your browser and deleted when you uninstall the Extension. Subtitle/comment content is not persistently stored.

Data Security

All network requests are transmitted over HTTPS.

What We Never Do

  • Never sell, rent, or trade your data;
  • Never use data for advertising, profiling, or anything unrelated to the Extension's functionality;
  • Never collect data in the background — all processing is initiated by your explicit action.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this document and the "Effective Date" at the top.
